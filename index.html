Roofing Warning Signs

Know when to request service.

These signals often indicate it is time to bring in an independent specialist for a professional assessment and next-step plan.

  • Recurring leaks or interior water staining
  • Missing shingles after high winds
  • Soft spots, sagging, or daylight in the attic
  • Heavy granule loss in gutters
  • Energy costs rising without another clear cause
