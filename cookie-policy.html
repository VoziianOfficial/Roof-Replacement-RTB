What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files that may be stored on your browser or device when you visit a website. Similar technologies may also be used, such as local storage, pixels, and analytics tools.

Why We Use Cookies

We may use cookies and related technologies to:

  • Support core website functions
  • Remember user preferences where applicable
  • Measure traffic and website performance
  • Improve form functionality and user experience

Types of Cookies

Essential Cookies

These cookies help the website function properly, including navigation, form handling, and basic security features.

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors use the website, which pages are visited, and how site performance can be improved.

Functional Cookies

Functional cookies may remember settings or interface behaviors so the site works more consistently across visits.

How to Control Cookies

Most browsers allow you to manage cookies through browser settings. You can usually block, limit, or delete cookies if you prefer.

Effect of Disabling Cookies

Disabling cookies may affect how some pages or features function, including form submission and saved preferences.

Relationship to Privacy Policy

Information collected through cookies may relate to information described in our Privacy Policy.
