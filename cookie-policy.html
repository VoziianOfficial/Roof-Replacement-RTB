Privacy Policy

How Roof Replacement RTB collects, uses, and protects your information.

This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected through our website, contact forms, service inquiries, and general communication.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information that you provide directly through forms, estimate requests, contact messages, or other website interactions. This may include your name, email address, phone number, property address, and details related to your roofing needs.

2. How We Use Information

Information may be used to respond to inquiries, provide service estimates, schedule inspections, communicate about roofing services, improve website experience, and maintain business records related to client communication.

3. Website Usage Data

We may collect standard website usage information such as browser type, device type, approximate location, pages visited, and session activity. This information helps us understand how visitors use the site and improve performance.

4. Contact Forms

When you submit a contact or estimate form, the information you enter may be used to follow up with you regarding your request, proposed services, scheduling, or customer support related to roofing work.

5. Sharing Of Information

We do not sell personal information through this website. Information may be shared only when necessary to operate the business, respond to legitimate service requests, comply with legal obligations, or work with service providers supporting website operations.

6. Data Protection

We take reasonable steps to protect submitted information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, no internet-based transmission or storage method can be guaranteed as completely secure.

7. Third-Party Links And Tools

Our website may contain third-party tools, map embeds, external links, or other services. Those third-party providers may have their own privacy practices, which we encourage users to review separately.

8. Your Choices

You may choose not to submit personal information through website forms. You may also contact us directly if you have questions about how your submitted information is handled.

9. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect website changes, service changes, legal requirements, or internal business practices.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your communication with Roof Replacement RTB, please visit our Contact page.
