Why Inspection Matters

Good decisions start with accurate roof information.

An inspection helps identify surface wear, structural concerns, moisture risk, installation issues, storm impact, and maintenance priorities.

It is especially useful before buying a home, planning repairs, or deciding whether full replacement is actually necessary.

Inspection Use Cases

  • Home purchase or sale support
  • Leak source review
  • Storm or hail assessment
  • Maintenance planning
  • Repair vs replacement decision-making
