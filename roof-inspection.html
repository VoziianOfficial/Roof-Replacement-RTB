Signs homeowners may notice

When to request an inspection.

Storm events

Hail, wind, or debris impact that warrants a documented review.

Age milestones

Roofs reaching ±15–20 years benefit from professional evaluation.

Energy spikes

Heating or cooling costs rise with suspected roof or insulation issues.

Visible wear

Curling, granule loss, or cracked flashing seen from the ground.

Insurance or sale

Requested documentation for underwriting or disclosure.

Maintenance strategy

Routine inspection to align with planned repairs or upgrades.

What inspection includes

Detail-rich reports.

  • Exterior and interior condition review
  • Leak and moisture detection
  • Flashing, gutter, and penetration check
  • Roof deck and ventilation assessment
  • Report with findings, photos, and recommendations

Reporting cadence varies by provider.

FAQ

Inspection concerns.

Is inspection always needed before repair?

Often. Providers want to diagnose the situation before proposing repairs.

Do you offer drone inspections?

Some providers include aerial imaging; mention your preference in the request.

Is there a cost?

Fees depend on provider and scope; clarify before scheduling.

Can inspections lead to insurance claims?

Yes. Inspectors can document damage for claims when warranted.
