Service explanation

Installation readiness simplified.

We handle the intake so you can compare providers for new construction or remodel roofs. Share project size, materials, and desired completion so local experts can respond accurately.

Independent providers handle permits, site prep, and crew management.

Signs homeowners may notice

Indicators that installation is next.

Roof age

Systems near the end of their life or with repeated repairs.

Material upgrades

Desire for higher-performing shingles or new aesthetics.

Structural changes

Major additions or roofline redesigns require fresh installs.

Persistent leaks

Continuous patchwork signals replacement is more cost-effective.

Energy concerns

Poor insulation or ventilation prompting full replacement.

Insurance incentives

Claim settlements that cover upgrades after storm events.

What installation includes

Comprehensive project components.

  • Material selection guidance and budgeting
  • Deck inspection and reinforcement
  • Underlayment, flashing, and ventilation strategy
  • Premium shingle, metal, or architectural systems
  • Final walkthrough and warranty information

Final scope is defined by the independent provider handling your project.

FAQ

Installation clarity.

Do you perform installations?

No. We introduce you to independent providers who handle the installation end-to-end.

How do providers coordinate with builders?

Providers can collaborate with your GC or builder; mention that in your request.

Are materials included?

Providers specify materials in their proposals; confirm coverage before signing.

What about permits?

Most providers handle permits as part of the installation scope.
