Signs homeowners may notice

When urgent repairs are likely needed.

Water stains

Ceiling discoloration or water drops after rainfall.

Shingle failure

Granules in gutters, cracked edges, or blown-off pieces.

Flashing gaps

Exposed seams near chimneys or wall transitions.

Attic moisture

Visible mold, damp insulation, or rusted fasteners.

Loose gutters

Fasteners pulling away from roof edges.

Storm damage

Missing material after hail or high winds.

What repair includes

Focused scope, clear expectations.

  • Diagnosis of leak source and condition
  • Shingle, flashing, and underlayment repairs
  • Flashing integrity and sealant replacement
  • Roof-to-wall system matching and cleanup
  • Post-repair inspection confirmation

Scope specifics depend on provider assessment.

Homeowner guidance

Preparation tips.

Document the issue

Take photos and note when leaks occur.

Share roof history

Mention previous repairs or warranties.

Clear access

Keep exterior and attic areas accessible for inspection.

FAQ and concerns

Quick answers.

Will a provider try to sell me a replacement instead?

Providers will assess and recommend based on condition; you can decide if repair or replacement fits your goals.

How soon can someone respond?

Response depends on location and urgency. We highlight urgent issues for faster prioritization.

Are estimates free?

Many providers offer complimentary assessments; confirm directly with the provider.

Should I contact insurance?

Yes, if the damage is storm-related. Providers can assist with documentation.
