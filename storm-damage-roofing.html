Signs homeowners may notice

Storm damage indicators.

Missing shingles

Visible roof area where shingles were blown off.

Granule loss

Clogged gutters and downspouts after hail.

Structural dents

Dents or bruising on metal flashing or vents.

Interior leaks

Water intrusion after storm events.

Insurance requests

Insurer requests inspection documentation.

Debris impact

Branches or hail causing obvious roof damage.

FAQ

Storm damage concerns.

Do providers work with insurance adjusters?

Yes, many coordinate reviews and provide documentation.

How soon can they respond?

Response time depends on storm severity; urgent cases are prioritized.

Is it safe to wait?

Delays can worsen damage. Requesting a match quickly is advised.

Are temporary repairs included?

Providers may offer tarps or boarding until final repairs are scheduled.
