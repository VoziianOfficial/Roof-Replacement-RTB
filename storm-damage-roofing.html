When This Category Fits

Situations that often align with weather-related roofing requests.

Storm damage requests are best when a recent weather event changed the roof condition and you need fast documentation, clearer urgency, and an informed next step.

Recent hail or wind

Useful when severe weather may have affected the roof shortly before the request.

Debris impact concern

Useful when objects or branches may have reached the roof surface.

Post-storm leak signs

Useful when roof issues appear after a recent weather event.

Time-sensitive context

Useful when urgency needs to be communicated clearly after weather exposure.

What To Prepare

Useful information before submitting a storm-related request.

Document when the event happened, what changed afterward, and whether there is an active leak or urgent exposure. Fresh detail is especially valuable on storm-related requests.

Date or timeframe of the weather event

Visible changes noticed after the event

Whether there is current leak or urgency

Property address and request timing
