About The Platform

RTB Roof Atlas is built to help homeowners make better roofing request decisions.

The platform is designed around category clarity, request preparation, and a calmer path through common roofing-related situations.

Platform Storyline

Why RTB exists and how users move through the platform.

01

Roofing needs often start with uncertainty

Users frequently know there is a concern, but not which request category fits best.

02

RTB organizes that uncertainty into pathways

The platform groups common roof situations into clearer request directions.

03

Users review notes before submitting

Category pages and planning notes help make the request more informed.

04

Next steps depend on routing and provider review

Execution, timing, and final scope always depend on the provider connected to the request.

What RTB Is / What RTB Is Not

Important role boundaries users should understand clearly.

What RTB Is

RTB is an informational and request-routing platform built to help users compare roofing-related pathways and prepare stronger requests.

What RTB Is Not

RTB is not presented as a direct roofing contractor, a final pricing engine, or a guaranteed provider-acceptance mechanism.
