About The Platform

RTB Roof Atlas is built to make roofing request decisions feel clearer, calmer, and more structured.

The platform is designed around category clarity, request preparation, and a more thoughtful path through common roofing-related situations.

RTB Roof Atlas is an informational and request-routing platform. It is not presented as a direct roofing contractor or final pricing engine.

What RTB Is / What RTB Is Not

Clear role boundaries make the platform easier to trust and understand.

What RTB Is

RTB is an informational and request-routing platform that helps users compare roofing-related pathways and prepare stronger next-step requests.

What RTB Is Not

RTB is not presented as a direct roofing contractor, a final diagnosis tool, or a guaranteed provider-acceptance mechanism.
