Privacy Policy

How RTB Roof Atlas collects, uses, and protects information.

This policy explains how information may be collected through the website, the request form, and general site interactions.

Privacy Policy Cookie Policy Terms Of Service

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information you submit through the request form or other site inputs. This may include your name, email address, phone number, property address, and any details you share about your roof situation.

2. How We Use Information

Information may be used to respond to your request, improve the website experience, and support basic site operations. RTB Roof Atlas is an informational platform and does not guarantee contractor availability, pricing, or scheduling.

3. Website Usage Data

We may collect standard website usage information such as browser type, device type, approximate location, pages visited, and session activity. This information helps us understand how visitors use the site and improve performance.

4. Contact Forms

When you submit the request form, the information you enter may be used to follow up on your request and clarify the category or details you provided.

5. Sharing Of Information

We do not sell personal information through this website. Information may be shared only when necessary to operate the website, respond to legitimate requests, comply with legal obligations, or work with service providers supporting site operations.

6. Data Protection

We take reasonable steps to protect submitted information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, no internet-based transmission or storage method can be guaranteed as completely secure.

7. Third-Party Links And Tools

Our website may contain third-party tools, map embeds, external links, or other services. Those third-party providers may have their own privacy practices, which we encourage users to review separately.

8. Your Choices

You may choose not to submit personal information through website forms. You may also contact us directly if you have questions about how your submitted information is handled.

9. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect website changes, service changes, legal requirements, or internal business practices.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please use the Request page.
