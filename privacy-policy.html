Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how RoofMatch Advisory collects, uses, stores, and shares information submitted through this website.

Last Updated: March 16, 2026 Related: Cookie Policy

Quick overview

RoofMatch Advisory is a homeowner-focused lead-generation platform. We help connect users with independent local roofing service providers and do not directly perform roofing work.

Homeowner requests Independent providers Privacy first

Who We Are

RoofMatch Advisory is a U.S. lead-generation platform designed to help homeowners connect with independent local roofing service providers. The site does not directly perform roof replacement, roof repair, roof installation, roof inspection, or other roofing work.

Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to information collected when you visit the website, complete a contact form, request quotes, call us, email us, or otherwise interact with our pages and features.

Information We Collect

We may collect information such as:

  • Name, phone number, and email address
  • ZIP code, property address, or service area details
  • Service request details you submit through forms
  • Basic device, browser, and usage information

How We Use Information

We may use information to:

  • Review and route your request
  • Connect you with independent roofing providers
  • Respond to inquiries and support requests
  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Maintain records and prevent misuse of the platform

How Information May Be Shared

Information submitted through the site may be shared with independent contractors, lead-handling partners, or service providers involved in reviewing and responding to your request.

Independent Contractors

Contractors or service providers contacted through RoofMatch Advisory are independent businesses. Homeowners are responsible for verifying licensing, insurance, and qualifications before hiring any provider.

Calls, Texts, and Contact Consent

By submitting a request or providing your contact information, you agree that you may be contacted regarding your inquiry by phone, email, or text message where permitted by law.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the platform, route requests, maintain records, and comply with legal obligations.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect submitted information. However, no online system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Your Choices

You may contact us to request updates, corrections, or questions about information submitted through the platform, subject to applicable legal limitations.

Cookies and Tracking

The website may use cookies and related technologies to support website performance and improve usability. Please review our Cookie Policy for more information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
